Shared World Fix

Follow these steps to fix connection issues.

Step 1: Browser Settings
Copy and paste this URL into your address bar: chrome://flags/#enable-webrtc-hide-local-ips-with-mdns Change Anonymize local IPs to Disabled.
Step 2: Restart
Click the Relaunch button at the bottom of the screen.
Step 1: Browser Settings
Copy and paste this URL into your address bar (Chrome or Edge): chrome://flags/#enable-webrtc-hide-local-ips-with-mdns Change Anonymize local IPs to Disabled and restart the browser.
Step 2: Network Profile Optional
Go to Settings > Network & Internet. Click your Wi-Fi and make sure it is set to Private.

What is the problem?

Your browser usually hides your local IP address to protect your privacy. While this is good for safety, it stops the game from finding other players on the same network.

By following the steps above, you allow the game to "see" your connection so you can join the shared world.
